Feature Verification Checklist

Use this page as the manual test companion for desktop, mobile, voice, editing, history, and PWA behavior.

Core Input

  • Type `2+3` and confirm result `5`.
  • Press `AC` and confirm expression clears cleanly.
  • Use `DEL` to remove one character at a time.
  • Use keyboard digits/operators on desktop when not editing.

Scientific Math

  • Check `sqrt(81)` gives `9`.
  • Check `log(100)` gives `2`.
  • Check `ln(e)` gives `1`.
  • Check `5!` gives `120`.
  • Check `17% of 330` gives `56.1`.

Angle Modes

  • In `RAD`, confirm `sin(pi/2)` gives `1`.
  • Toggle to `DEG`, confirm `sin(30)` gives `0.5`.
  • Check `cos(60)` gives `0.5` in `DEG`.
  • Check `atan(1)` gives `45` in `DEG`.

Voice Input

  • Try `log of 10` and confirm result `1`.
  • Try `square root of 2` and confirm no stale manual display remains.
  • Try `2 raised to 2` and confirm result `4`.
  • Try `cube of 9` and confirm result `729`.
  • Try an invalid phrase and confirm old results do not remain on screen.

Edit Mode

  • Tap `Edit`, place the cursor in the middle, and type.
  • Use `Delete` and confirm it removes at the caret position.
  • Press `Enter` to evaluate and exit edit mode.
  • Confirm desktop shortcuts do not interfere while editing.

History and Memory

  • Confirm successful calculations add entries to history.
  • Confirm failed voice parses do not create fake success history entries.
  • Use `M+`, `M-`, `MR`, and `MC` and verify memory indicator updates.
  • Use `ANS` and confirm it inserts the previous answer.

Mobile Layout

  • Check portrait keypad order is natural on phone.
  • Check result stays readable and controls stay tappable.
  • Check history shifts below or alongside gracefully depending on width.
  • Check no overflow/crowding in the display area.

PWA and Updates

  • Deploy changes and refresh once.
  • Confirm updated JS/CSS loads after redeploy.
  • If installed, reopen the app and confirm the new build appears.
  • Confirm stale parser behavior does not persist after refresh.
Best workflow: run calculator-tests.html, run voice-parser-tests.html, then use this checklist for live desktop/mobile verification.
